Nippon India AIF
UI/UX Case Study

Role
UI/UX Designer
Platform
Desktop & Mobile Web
Industry
Financial Services
Visit Live Website
The Challenge

The old website looked outdated and didn't reflect the scale and trust of a global brand like Nippon. It needed a fresh, modern look that feels world-class and makes it easy for anyone to understand what they offer.

Our Process

We researched competitor websites and studied how top global investment firms present themselves, then created wireframes to shape a design direction that gives Nippon India AIF a truly global identity.

How We Resolved It

We redesigned the layout with a cleaner structure and bolder visual hierarchy. Brand colors were used more confidently across sections, giving the site a premium, global feel.

The Outcome

The redesigned website now reflects the scale and credibility of a global financial brand. Investors can explore complex fund offerings with ease.

Supreme Group
UI/UX Case Study

Role
UI/UX Designer
Platform
Desktop & Mobile Web
Industry
Manufacturing & Industrial Materials
Visit Live Website
The Challenge

Supreme Group works behind the scenes for big industries like automotive, textiles, and filtration, but their website didn't reflect the scale and innovation of their work. It felt outdated, making it hard for visitors to understand what the company actually does.

Our Process

We researched how leading global industrial and manufacturing brands present themselves online, then mapped out the key industries Supreme Group serves and built early wireframes to organize content in a digestible flow.

How We Resolved It

We gave the website a cleaner, more modern layout with strong visuals. Technical information was simplified into clear sections, making it easy for both industry experts and new visitors to understand their expertise.

The Outcome

The new website positions Supreme Group as a global leader in nonwoven innovation, communicating technical expertise with clarity and confidence.

Innogent
UI/UX Case Study

Role
UI/UX Designer
Platform
Desktop & Mobile Web
Industry
Technology / Software Development
Visit Live Website
The Challenge

Innogent offers a wide range of technical services — React, AWS, DevOps, AI, Big Data — but the website struggled to communicate this clearly. The site felt cluttered and didn't convey Innogent's expertise or scale as a software development partner.

Our Process

We researched how leading software and IT service companies structure their websites and built early wireframes to organize all of Innogent's core services into a clear, digestible flow.

How We Resolved It

We restructured the website with a clean, modern layout that highlights each service without overwhelming the visitor. Technical jargon was simplified into clear, benefit-driven language.

The Outcome

The redesigned website makes it easy for potential clients to quickly understand Innogent's full range of expertise. The site now feels modern, credible, and aligned with the technology partner the company truly is.
